Who are we
Transcend Imagination.
We are a team of professional visual artists and machine learning researchers. Our mission is to revolutionize AI-Generated art by blending technology with human expertise in the creative process.
Natural Intuition.
Our designers build, treat and tailor our own datasets. We carefully find thousands of datapoints which we blend in with our own original creations and filters to breathe life and originality into our models.
Supernatural Insight.
Our machine learning experts use bleeding edge AI to generate extraordinary interpretations from our datasets, engineering exclusive pieces and new visions of the world.
